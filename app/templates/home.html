About Ivan's Kitchen

Your Needs

Every dog is different, there is no accurate answer to what your dog should be eating. We believe your preferences should be assessed and formed to fit your dog's needs. We strive to become close and personal with each and every one of you and your dogs.

Our Recipe

With our meals we assure a nice simple recipe with fresh & organic additives per request. We have spent much time researching, speaking with Vet Clinics, Holistic Vet Clinics, dog nutritionists, and personal opinions of pet owners. So many opinions have helped create this diverse way of feeding your dog.

Nutrients

Your dog's diet should contain proper nutrients proportions- our recipe assures the correct intake of nutrients. We use all organic 50% Vegetables, 40% Protein, and 10% Grain. We also offer Grain Free meals. Our food is mainly raw and is not dried. Raw veggies are better because cooking them can cause loss of nutrients.
